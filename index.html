Project
Quiet Light Villa
Year
2022
Category
Interior
Location
Hangzhou, China

Harmonizing tradition and modernity.

This interior renovation of a traditional villa in Hangzhou focuses on the interaction between wooden materials and diffused light. By stripping away years of unnecessary additions, the original spatial proportions were restored and enhanced with minimal architectural elements.

The design employs a monochromatic palette of warm grays and natural wood, creating a seamless flow between the interior and the surrounding landscape. Soft light filters through custom-made linen screens, echoing the traditional paper windows of local architecture.
